## Recommended Reading

A focus on depth over breadth. Here are some notable books worth exploring:

### Fiction

- **The Remains of the Day** by Kazuo Ishiguro
- **Station Eleven** by Emily St. John Mandel
- **The Overstory** by Richard Powers

### Non-Fiction

- **The Art of Living** by Thich Nhat Hanh
- **Atomic Habits** by James Clear
- **The Design of Everyday Things** by Don Norman

### Philosophy & Essays

- **Meditations** by Marcus Aurelius
- **The Art of War** by Sun Tzu
- **Essays** by Montaigne

## Reading Philosophy

Effective reading involves:

- **Slow reading** - Taking time to absorb and reflect
- **Margin notes** - Engaging actively with the text
- **Re-reading** - Returning to great books
- **Variety** - Mixing genres and perspectives

## Conclusion

Reading is a conversation across time and space, connecting us with ideas and perspectives from different eras and cultures.
